Guess The Song
Game on "Microbit"

At Guess The Song Project we are playing a fun game of two players to guess the song which is related to the melody played.

Game Strategy

Player one has the music play and quizzes player two on their music knowledge

  • Play tone for few seconds
  • If guess was right, player one provides them the two points by clicking A button
  • If guess was wrong, they get -1 point by clicking B button
  • If can't guess, they can do pass by clicking the Logo with no points gained
  • After playing all 6 tones, the game declares the total score
